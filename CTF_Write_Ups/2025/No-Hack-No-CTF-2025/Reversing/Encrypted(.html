## Encrypted(?

### 490

This suspicious executable encrypted my files.I don't know how to recover. Can you help me recover them? They included some of my favorite memes and an image containing the flag you need for this challenge. I spent like half an hour hand-drawing it, and now it's gone 😭😭😭

For this challenge we are given :

ls -la  
total 1676

drwxr-xr-x. 1 t1110 t1110    1016 jul 11 20:14 ..  
drwxrwxr-x. 1 t1110 t1110     120 jul  4 20:06 encrypted_memes  
-rwxr--r--. 1 t1110 t1110   84120 jul  6 13:58 encrypter  
-rw-r--r--. 1 t1110 t1110     507 jul 14 19:57 flag2_enc.txt

This is an encrypted flag and some photos with the original encrypter. After seeing the file it looks like an XOR encryption but which I could not following because it was done in-memory.
